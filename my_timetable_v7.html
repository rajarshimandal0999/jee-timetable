⚙️ Study Mode Selector

Choose your operating mode based on how much risk you want to carry into school vs JEE. Each mode has a different strategy for exam periods, vacations, and normal weeks.

🛡️ Conservative Mode — "Zero School Risk"

Night before exam: 2–3 hrs school prep, then sleep. Live class skipped if exam is next morning. Exam morning: 30-min last scan. Exam day evening: one recorded lecture only, no pressure. Buffer day after: half school review, half JEE. You score 70–80% in school without it costing meaningful JEE time — because you've budgeted for it properly.

📅 Normal Weeks Normal

  • → Attend all live Lakshya classes as scheduled
  • → 1 backlog lecture per weekday if energy permits
  • → Full Sunday backlog block (3–4 lecs)
  • → Full tuition attendance every day
  • → 8 hrs sleep protected always

📋 Exam Weeks Exam

  • → Night before: 2–3 hrs school prep → sleep by 10:30 PM
  • → Exam morning: 30-min subject scan, nothing else
  • → Skip live Lakshya if exam is next day morning
  • → Exam day evening: 1 recorded lec only, no backlog
  • → Buffer day: 50% school review + 50% JEE recorded

🏖️ Vacations Vacation

  • → 5–6 backlog lectures per day (max assault)
  • → 2 Lakshya recorded sessions daily
  • → Full DPP daily (not just 2/week)
  • → School prep for upcoming exams: 1 hr/day
  • → Sleep by midnight, wake 7 AM

⚡ JEE Risks

  • Backlog target may slip during all 3 exam periods
  • Missing live classes creates recording debt
  • Phase 2 (Sep) may start with incomplete backlog
  • Momentum breaks are harder to recover from

📚 School Benefits

  • Strong board exam prep — marks well protected (70–80%)
  • Low stress during school exam weeks
  • No conflict between school and coaching calendars
  • Sustainable long-term — no burnout from overreach

⚖️ Liberal Mode — "The Recommended Path"

Night before exam: exactly 45 min school prep, then stop. Live class is attended regardless. Exam morning: 15-min formula scan only. Exam day evening: back to normal JEE schedule. Buffer day: one extra recorded lecture, zero school review. School scores land 65–80%. The 45-min cap is strict — you set a timer, when it rings you close the book.

📅 Normal Weeks Normal

  • → All live Lakshya classes attended
  • → 1–2 backlog lecs/weekday + full Sunday blocks
  • → DPP both Wed and Fri as scheduled
  • → Revision 50 min nightly after recorded
  • → Weekly target: 12 Cl.12 + 8 backlog lecs

📋 Exam Weeks Exam

  • → Night before: 45 min school prep (timer), then sleep
  • → Exam morning: 15-min formula scan only — nothing more
  • → Live class: always attended, no exceptions
  • → Exam day evening: full JEE schedule resumes
  • → Buffer day: 1 recorded + skip backlog, no school review

🏖️ Vacations Vacation

  • → 4–5 backlog lectures per day
  • → Both Lakshya sessions daily (live or recorded)
  • → Full DPP daily
  • → Target: clear 30–40 backlog lecs per vacation week
  • → 1 rest day per vacation block — protect it

⚡ JEE Upside

  • Backlog target achievable by Aug without rushing
  • Live class continuity maintained all year
  • Phase transitions (Aug→Sep→Nov) go smoothly
  • Vacation sprints compensate for exam week losses

📚 School Risks

  • 45-min cap is strict — needs discipline to not overrun
  • Some school exams will feel underprepared
  • Marks will be 65–80%, not 90%+ range
  • Requires a timer and hard stop mentality

🔥 Radical Mode — "JEE Maximum"

Night before exam: 20-min scan of formula sheet, then sleep. That's it. Live class always attended the same day. Exam morning: zero prep — you already scanned last night. Exam day evening: full JEE schedule, no adjustment. Buffer day: treated as a normal JEE day. School exam is a speed bump you drive over without slowing down.

📅 Normal Weeks Normal

  • → Live Lakshya: non-negotiable every single day
  • → 2–3 backlog lecs/weekday evening if energy allows
  • → Full Sunday: 6–7 backlog lectures total
  • → DPP every day, not just Wed/Fri
  • → Weekly target: 12 Cl.12 + 10+ backlog

📋 Exam Weeks Exam

  • → Night before: 20-min formula sheet scan — stop there
  • → Exam morning: zero additional prep, eat and go
  • → Live class: attended regardless — no exceptions
  • → Exam day evening: full normal JEE schedule
  • → Buffer day: treated as a completely normal JEE day

🏖️ Vacations Vacation

  • → 6–8 backlog lectures per day — full blast
  • → Wake 6 AM, study until 10–11 PM
  • → Clear 50+ backlog lecs per vacation week
  • → Zero rest days during vacation — rest after JEE
  • → Aim to finish ALL backlog before Puja vacation

⚡ JEE Upside

  • Backlog fully cleared before August — ahead of schedule
  • Phase 2 starts from a position of massive strength
  • Maximum exposure to Lakshya curriculum
  • Vacation periods become decisive advantage

📚 School Risks — HIGH

  • School marks drop significantly — 50–65% likely
  • Risk of detention if attendance or marks fall too low
  • Bengali and English board prep gets almost zero time
  • Burnout risk is real if vacation pace is sustained too long

  • 8:00 PMSet a 45-min timer. Open school notes.
  • 8:45 PMTimer rings → close the book. Done.

  • 10:30 PMSleep. Full stop.
  • Morning15-min formula scan only. Eat breakfast.

  • 9:30 PMOpen formula sheet. Read once. Close it.
  • 9:50 PMDone. That's your entire prep.
  • 10:00 PMWatch one recorded at 1.5x if you want
  • 11:00 PMSleep.
  • MorningZero additional prep. Eat, go, write, return.
